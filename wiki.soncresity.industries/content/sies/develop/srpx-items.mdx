<Callout title="Caution" type="warning">
  This page contains **all Items in all of their theme variants** resulting in 9 (themes) \* 37 (items) = 333 textures.
  If you are viewing this page over mobile data and not wifi, we advise you to close this page to avoid high data consumption.
</Callout>

In around version 2.1.0 of **SI: Essentials** we began experimenting by adding 4 Serephix Items and using them internally.

Now, in **SI: Essentials** 2.3.0 we have introduced [theming](/sies/develop/theming) for your items but already implemented this in our own items (37 in total as of writing)

You can use all of those in GUIs (as shown in most examples [here](/sies/develop/utils/slots)) which will appear differently for each user, depending on their currently selected theme.

Below you can find the texture for each item and its theme.
